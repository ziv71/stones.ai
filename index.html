    About the project

    How the stone scanner turns photos into meaning.

    Scroll through the workflow to see how the app transforms a stone image into a geological profile, ecological insight, and design inspiration.

    01

    Upload or select a sample

    The experience starts by choosing a real stone photo from the gallery or dropping your own image into the scanner.

    02

    Analyze visual signals

    The app evaluates color, contrast, brightness, and texture to create a visual signature for the sample.

    03

    Match to curated profiles

    That signature is compared against curated stone profiles to find the closest geological match.

    04

    Reveal stone context

    The result page explains the rock type, formation, ecology, and artistic uses to make each stone informative.
